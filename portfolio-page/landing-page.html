Welcome to Wonka Chocolate

Story

Charlie Bucket, a poor paperboy, wants to visit the Willy Wonkas famous chocolate factory.

He finds one of five hidden "Golden Tickets" in chocolate Wonka Bars and wins a factory tour together with his Grandpa and the other winners.

The next day, Wonka greets the ticket winners and leads them inside. The factory includes a candy land with a river of chocolate, edible mushrooms, gummy bears, candy canes, chocolate and more sweets. They see Wonka's workers, small men known as Oompa-Loompas.

As the other winners are lost because of various accidents on tour only Charlie and his Grandpa remain.

Charlie asks about the fate of the other four kids, and Wonka swiftly assures him that that they will be restored to normal.

Wonka declares Charlie as the winner, and he reveals that the prize is actually the factory.

He had created the contest to find an heir worthy enough to be, and so Charlie and his family can immediately move in.

Charlie will therefore take over the factory business when Wonka retires.

Recipe

INGREDIENTS

  • 1⁄2 cup margarine, softened
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1⁄2 box powdered sugar
  • 1 1⁄2 packages graham crackers
  • 1 (12 ounce) package chocolate chips
  • 2 tablespoons margarine or 1/4 cup milk

DIRECTIONS

  • Combine margarine, peanut butter, powdered sugar and graham crackers.
  • Press mixture in (8"x8") pan.
  • Melt chips and 2 tablespoons of margarine or milk.
  • Spread over graham cracker crust.
  • Refrigerate.
  • Cut when cool.
